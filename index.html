About Us

Shan Sons Engineering Works is a company dedicated to serving the nation with high-quality auto parts, textile parts, and tractor parts. We have been in business since 1989. We have been serving our customers ever since. We started our business as a small unit. Then with the help of ALLAH ALMIGHTY, we have been prospering and now we have a state-of-the-art facility. We are proud to be serving our customers and our nation with the dedication of the best quality parts, and timely deliveries.

Our Moto

Manufactures of auto parts, sheet metal parts, tractor parts, textile parts, and all kinds of machine parts.
